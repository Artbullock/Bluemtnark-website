About Art

A Career Built on Leadership, Service, and Relationships That Last

"We're here to support you. Your business matters to us, and our goal is to create a long-term partnership built on trust, reliability, and a relationship that feels as comfortable as working with a friend."

Art Bullock's path to real estate was forged through decades of genuine leadership — from growing up in a large family in Arkansas, to serving as a U.S. Army Sergeant at 21, to rising through the ranks of a Fortune 500 company where he hired, trained, and managed multimillion-dollar operations.

That foundation of service, discipline, and community is what drives Blue Mountain of Arkansas, LLC. Art brings a national network of buyers, sellers, lenders, and capital partners together — not as transactions, but as relationships built to last.

He is a devoted husband, father, and grandfather, an active leader in his local church and Christian men's ministry, and a passionate advocate for underserved communities. When Art says he cares — he means it.

Do You Qualify?
Art pre-screens all borrowers and deal operators before making introductions. Here's what strong candidates typically look like:
  • ✓
    Credit Score 700+
    Strong personal credit history demonstrates reliability and lender confidence.
  • ✓
    10–15% Down Payment Available
    Liquidity to support the loan and demonstrate skin-in-the-game.
  • ✓
    Relevant Experience or Qualified Partner
    Track record in the asset class, or a verified experienced operator as co-GP.
  • ✓
    Clear Purpose & Exit Strategy
    Defined use of funds, repayment plan, and realistic project timeline.
  • ✓
    Sufficient Liquidity
    Reserves to support the loan through project completion or stabilization.
